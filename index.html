Meet Our Founder

Sakalesh Kadadi

With over two decades of dedication to Ayurvedic wellness, Sakalesh Kadadi founded Pranarogya Herbal with a vision to bring authentic, organic products to modern households.

His journey began in the traditional herb gardens of Karnataka, where he learned the ancient art of identifying and processing medicinal plants from experienced Ayurvedic practitioners.

Today, Sakalesh continues to personally oversee every aspect of our production process, ensuring that each product maintains the highest standards of purity and potency.
